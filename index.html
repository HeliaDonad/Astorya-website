Plans & Pricing

Lost in the starry sky? We’ll guide you back.

What is Astorya?
Astorya is a digital platform where you can preserve your memories by reserving a star in a virtual sky. Fill your star with messages, photos, and audio, and share your legacy with your loved ones.
Is Astorya free to use?
Astorya offers a basic plan with limited features for free and a premium plan with unlimited stars and memories.
What kind of memories can I add to my star?
You can add text, photos, personal messages, and audio recordings.
Can I share my star with someone?
Yes, you can invite family and friends to view your star and its memories. You can choose to keep it private or share it with the world.
