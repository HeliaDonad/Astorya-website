Lost in the starry sky? We’ll guide you back.

What is Astorya?
Astorya is a digital platform where you can preserve your memories by reserving a star in a virtual sky. Fill your star with messages, photos, and audio, and share your legacy with your loved ones.
Is Astorya free to use?
Astorya offers a basic plan with limited features for free and a premium plan with unlimited stars and memories.
What kind of memories can I add to my star?
You can add text, photos, personal messages, and audio recordings.
Can I share my star with someone?
Yes, you can invite family and friends to view your star and its memories. You can choose to keep it private or share it with the world.
What is the time capsule feature?
The time capsule feature allows you to set a future date for when specific memories will be unlocked and shared with your selected contacts.
When is the app launching?
June 2025 :)
Can I change the unlock date?
Yes, you can edit or remove the unlock date as long as the memory hasn’t been opened yet.
Is my data safe with Astorya?
Yes, your privacy is our priority. We use encryption to ensure your data is protected and accessible only to you and the people you choose to share it with.
Can I delete my star or account?
Yes, you can delete your star or your entire account at any time. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
What happens if I lose access to my account?
If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking on "Forgot Password" and following the instructions.
Is Astorya available on all devices?
No, Astorya is currently only available as a mobile app for both iOS and Android.
