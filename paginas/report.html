Report a passing

To activate your loved one’s memorial star, please complete the steps below. This process ensures their memories are released with care, privacy, and respect.

  • Enter the unique code assigned to your loved one’s star
  • Upload a copy of the death certificate to confirm the passing
  • Once submitted, our team will review the information. If everything is in order, the star will be activated and their preserved memories will become visible in the shared starry sky
