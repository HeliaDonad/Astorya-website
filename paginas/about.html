Our mission

Our app helps people preserve memories in a personal and meaningful way, keeping a lasting connection with loved ones who have passed. Each star represents a tribute, holding the memories and continuing the story of the person who is no longer with us.

Our Vision

We believe that memories are not just fleeting moments, but enduring legacies that keep us connected long after we're gone. Our vision is to create a space where these memories can live on, offering a lasting bond that transcends time and keeps the stories of our loved ones alive.

The story behind astorya

We are three students from Thomas More University of Applied Sciences in Mechelen, united by a shared vision to make a lasting impact. Astorya was born from our desire to create something timeless — a digital space where stories, memories, and love can continue to shine long after we’re gone. We wanted to create a way for people to leave something meaningful behind — a way to share their love, wisdom, and memories with those who matter most.


Our platform allows everyone to preserve their legacy in a unique way, making sure that important moments, lessons, and connections are never lost. Astorya isn’t just about memories; it’s about connection, comfort, and creating a bridge between generations.


Guided by our passion for technology and storytelling, we built Astorya as a way to honor the past, celebrate the present, and inspire the future.


We believe that every life is like a star — unique, bright, and worthy of remembrance. And through Astorya, those stories will continue to light up the sky.
